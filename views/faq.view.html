Is Healora Health Insurance?

No. It is a direct pay network connecting doctors and patients together to remove up to 70% of the middle layer administrative costs.

How does it work with my health insurance?

If you can pay less for the procedure than your out of pocket costs using your deductible or if you want a specific doctor that is not in your network, then take a look at Healora.

Can I use Healora and my Health Insurance at the same time?

Of course. Healora is a directpay network so your insurance does not apply. In some cases it is less expensive to pay directly rather than use your insurance and pay the deductibles, copays and coinsurance.

Can anyone use Healora?

Yes. Healora is the non-insurance option. You decide which is the lowest cost paying direct or using your insurance and paying your deductible and copay.

How do I decide on my fee structure?

You establish your pricing based on the procedure. Healora suggests medicare + 20%. Healora will provide the CPT codes and medicare pricing so you will have a reference point. The more competitive your fees are, the faster your Direct Pay practice should grow. The sweet spot seems to be medicare + 20%. Remember that this is paid at the time of service. There is no balance billing or collections. Administrative costs associated with activities such as billing, producing insurance claim forms, coding diagnoses and procedures, referrals, authorizations, payment delays, EOB reviews, claim denials, re-submissions, collection risks, and other “managed care” costs are all eliminated. You can provide patients a fair price for services without the administrative hassles and bureaucracy. You have the legal right to charge a reduced rate for a particular medical service based on a “prompt pay.” You may change your pricing up or down at any time. Your price stays hidden from the public. Only our registered members see your specific pricing just like your other payor contracts.

Will insurance companies or Medicare have a problem?

Insurance payor contracts limit your practice from advertising prices publicly. They do this by having you agree that your lowest advertised price is the price they will reimburse. Healora only shares your cash prices with our registered member network so you do not violate any of those contracts.
