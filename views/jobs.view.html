Jr. Market Research Analyst:



40 hours/week. Job Interview Site: Santa Monica, CA. Email Resume to Kles Limited DBA HEalora at info@healora.com

  • Sacramento, California
  • Post Date: June 1, 2018

Job Description

Assist CEO with researching conditions in the health insurance market and utilizing the information to create marketing campaigns. Use Microsoft Word and Excel to create surveys, opinion polls, and questionnaires based on CEO’s design and specifications, and submit to CEO for review. Based on the results of said surveys, utilize Microsoft Word and PowerPoint to prepare reports summarizing healthcare providers’ prices and services and patients’ demographics, income, and healthcare needs. Submit reports to CEO for review. Assist CEO in analyzing the findings so that he may determine how to modify the company’s services and products to best suit the needs of the customer base. Assist in designing different promotional strategies and conducting feasibility studies to determine their practicality and viability. Use Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe InDesign to create flyers, posters, and other marketing materials, and launch social media campaigns under the CEO’s supervision. Under direction of the CEO, assist in measuring the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. Read trade literature to remain apprised of the latest trends and developments in the healthcare industry.

Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

The position requires a Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, or Advertising plus 1 year of experience in the job offered or a related position. Equivalent foreign degrees are acceptable. The position requires skills and knowledge in Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, Microsoft PowerPoint, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, social media platforms, and feasibility studies.
