Healora Benefits


Healora reduce medical procedure prices by removing the middlemen. Connecting doctors and patients directly removes all the costly administrative middle layers. Up to 70% savings passes to patients and employers. Healora is a direct pay cash network with access to the best doctors.

Healora removes all the costly middle layers between doctors and patients. This direct pay network removes up to 70% of the administrative costs of a medical procedure. Doctors name their direct to consumer price. Patients have transparency to make healthcare more affordable.

Healora’s Mission is to make healthcare more accessible for everyone. We are the “deductible buster”
